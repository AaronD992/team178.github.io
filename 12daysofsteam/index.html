12 Days of STEAM Activities

Welcome to 12 Days of STEAM! Starting on Feburary 1st, fun activites will be posted each day for you to engage in! Be sure to check back on this page each day to see that day's activity. Feel free to post a picture of your completed activity on social media and tag Team 178 too!

Activity of the Day: First activity will be posted Feburary 1st!

Materials List

Here are materials for each day so you know beforehand:
  • Day 1: Paper Airplanes
    • Paper
  • Day 2: Gravity Defying Snake
    • Tissue paper
    • Plastic ruler
    • Scissors
    • Something made of wool
    • A tin plate or tin lid
  • Day 3: Compass Experiment
    • Jumpo popsicle stick
    • Bowl of water
    • Compass
    • Transparent tape
    • Scissors
    • Paperclip
    • Styrofoam cup
    • Strong magnet
    • Pen
  • Day 4: Gingerbread Circuits
    • Lunch size paper bag
    • Markers or crayons to decorate
    • Copper tape
    • LED lights
    • 3 volt lithium batteries
    • Tape
    • Paperclips
    • Small stones
  • Day 5: Popsicle Snowflake
    • Colored markers
    • 4 popsicle sticks
    • Glue
    • 2 LED lights
    • Pieces of string/yarn
  • Day 6: Invisible Ink
    • Cotton/cue tips
    • Lemon
    • A bowl
    • White paper
    • A light/lamp
  • Day 7: DVD Hovercrafts
    • An old CD or DVD disc
    • A 9 inch balloon
    • A pop-top cap from a liquid soap bottle or water bottle
    • A hot glue gun
  • Day 8: Christmas Origami
    • Paper
  • Day 9: Home Dome
    • Toothpicks
    • Modeling clay
  • Day 10: Chemical Balloons
    • A packet of yeast
    • A clean, clear, plastic soda bottle
    • 1 teaspoon of sugar
    • Warm water
    • A small balloon
  • Day 11: Glow in the Dark Jello
    • Jello (light colored is best)
    • 16oz of tonic water
    • Stove
    • Liquid measuring cup
    • Small pot for boiling water
    • Mixing bowl
    • Refrigerator
    • Small table lamp with a fluorescent backlight
